Research Assistance

Learn about the collection
Read the Guide to the Vital Records Collection, which details the history of vital records-keeping in New York City, the scope of the collections, and additional resources for research. Still have questions? Contact us.

Still can't find a vital record?
Fill out the Vital Records Order Form, and we will conduct a search. If the record is located, we will digitize on demand. If not, we will provide a certified not found letter.
