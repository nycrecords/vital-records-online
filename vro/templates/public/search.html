By Name

Searching by name is in beta mode, and may not produce accurate results. Marriage licenses are not yet indexed by name, so no marriage licenses will be returned in name search results.
Search tips:

  • Certificate numbering restarts at one for each borough, every year.
  • Search by any spelling or pronunciation variations of names.
  • The year on the certificate represents the date the document was filed with the City agency, not necessarily the year of the birth, death, or marriage.
  • Use a range of years.
  • Use the mother’s maiden name to narrow searches on birth certificates.
  • Note: approximately 25% of all births before 1909 were not reported and never issued a birth certificate.
  • Marriage certificates and licenses use the brides maiden name not married name.
  • The date of birth or age of death is useful in determining the correct individual.
